🗑️ Delete Your Account & Data

Option 1: Delete ALL Data (Recommended)

In the app:

  1. Open RAI application
  2. Go to Settings tab
  3. Scroll to Data Management section
  4. Click "DELETE ALL DATA" button

This permanently deletes all your receipts, expenses, and reminders.

Option 2: Delete Only Your Account (Logout)

In the app:

  1. Open RAI application
  2. Tap the ☰ Menu icon (top left)
  3. Scroll down and click "Logout"

Your Firebase account will be disconnected. Local data remains on your device.

Option 3: Delete Everything by Uninstalling

Uninstall the application from your phone.

This immediately deletes all local receipts, expenses, and reminders.

What data is deleted?

  • ✅ All receipts and expense records
  • ✅ All reminders and notifications
  • ✅ Fixed expenses configuration
  • ✅ Your email from marketing list (on request)
