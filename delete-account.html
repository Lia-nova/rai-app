🗑️ Delete Your Account & Data

Option 1: Delete All Data (Recommended)

Uninstall the application from your phone.

This immediately deletes all your receipts, expenses, and reminders stored locally.

Option 2: Delete Only Your Account

If you created an account:

  1. Open the RAI application
  2. Go to Settings → Logout
  3. Your Firebase account will be disconnected

What data is deleted?

  • ✅ All receipts and expense records
  • ✅ All reminders and notifications
  • ✅ Fixed expenses configuration
