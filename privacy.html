Privacy Policy for RAI

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Email Collection and Communications

We collect email addresses for account creation and authentication purposes. With your explicit opt-in consent, we may also send you product updates, tips, and promotional communications. You can withdraw this consent at any time. We never share your email with third parties.

2. Data Collection and Storage

RAI stores all your receipts and expense records locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal spending information on external servers. Your receipt images and expense data remain private and secure on your device.

3. Permissions

The app requires camera access to capture receipts and storage access to save them. These permissions are used solely for the app's functionality and are not used to access any other data on your device.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted using platform-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

RAI uses Google ML Kit for text recognition from receipts. This processing happens on-device and no image data is sent to Google servers. For email authentication, we use Firebase Auth which complies with privacy standards.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete any receipt or expense record at any time
  • Withdraw marketing consent in app settings
  • Export your data in TXT/PDF format
  • Permanently remove all data by uninstalling the app

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Important: This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms & Conditions which include important disclaimers about data responsibility and limitation of liability.
